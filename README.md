## 🚧 What I'm building

**TUI with BubbleTea** — Learned TUI development the hard way. Turns out making a terminal app look good is a full-time job, and I already have one of those.

**Hot reloader in Go** — Because waiting for manual restarts during development is a form of suffering I refuse to accept.

**Custom split keyboard** — Assembling a split mechanical keyboard from sourced parts. It will either be the most ergonomic thing I've ever typed on, or an expensive lesson in hubris.
